PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: April 7, 2026

DATA WE COLLECT

DialIn is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Nickname — The name you choose to display on leaderboards. This is stored locally on your device and submitted to our database only when you choose to participate in Daily or Duel leaderboards.
  • Game Scores — Your scores are saved to participate in Daily and Duel leaderboards. No personal information is required.
  • Game Progress — Your level, streak, and game history are stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it.

LOCAL STORAGE

We use browser localStorage to save your preferences:

  • Game progress and level
  • Sound preferences
  • Nickname
  • Language preference
  • Daily challenge status

You can clear all local data at any time using the Reset button in the navigation bar.

SERVER DATA

When you participate in Daily challenges or Duels, we store:

  • Your chosen nickname
  • Your score and round details
  • Your color personality result

This data is public and visible on leaderboards. We do not collect email addresses, passwords, or any personally identifiable information.

THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

  • Google Fonts — We load Orbitron and Exo 2 fonts from Google Fonts. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudflare — Our game is hosted on Cloudflare Pages and uses Cloudflare D1 database. See Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

COOKIES

We do not use cookies. All preferences are stored in localStorage.

DATA DELETION

To delete your local data, use the Reset button (🔄) in the navigation bar. For server-side data (leaderboard entries), please contact us.

CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

DialIn is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13 beyond what is described above (nickname and game scores).

CHANGES

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date.

CONTACT

Questions about this policy? Reach us at the DialIn community.
